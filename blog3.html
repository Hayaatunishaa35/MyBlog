Events You Won’t Want to Miss this Fall at Birdland Jazz Club

If you are a jazz lover, no visit to New York City is complete without a visit to the Birdland Jazz Club. Since opening its doors in 1949, Birdland has hosted hundreds of the most talented jazz bands and singers. The club has a phenomenal lineup of incredibly gifted jazz artists this fall, so make sure you plan your New York trip around these sensational shows.


The Liebman, Copland and Alessi Quintet

Renowned jazz musicians David Liebman, Marc Copland, Ralph Alessi, Joey Baron, and Drew Gress can be experienced live from September 3 through September 7. This show is the first appearance for these performers as a quintet. Each musician in the Liebman, Copland and Alessi quintet is a talented bandleader, player, and composer. That means seeing them take the stage together will be an incredible treat for any jazz lover.


America’s Sweethearts

America has always favored this all-women jazz group since they hit the airwaves. You can hear renditions of your favorite classic numbers in a fresh, new way on September 10. America’s Sweethearts take the stage at 7 PM and are sure to provide an upbeat evening of fun that is perfect for the entire family.


Eric Comstock, Sean Smith, and Barbara Fasano

Birdland visitors are in for a treat on September 14 when renowned musician Eric Comstock takes the stage. He will be joined by the talented bassist Sean Smith and the legendary Barbara Fasano, who will be singing. Between the three musicians, you will be treated to beautiful, soulful songs from every era of the jazz industry.


Everybody Rise: A Resistance Cabaret

Perfect for all ages, Everybody Rise: A Resistance Cabaret is a concert reading that features classic songs. Each tune has been cleverly rewritten to reflect on the age of Trump, making for a fun and unforgettable reading. This show will be back by popular demand on September 24.
With such an incredible lineup of fall shows, it is no surprise that Birdland is one of the best jazz clubs in New York. If you are a jazz enthusiast, see incredible Jazz shows in New York at Birdland. View our calendar for our complete lineup and purchase tickets today.
